About Us

Mansoorah Group of Companies

Mansoorah Group of Companies (MGC) is a diversified business group operating across multiple sectors, including education, social welfare, construction, agriculture, dairy farming, import and export, energy distribution, and information technology.

With a commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainable growth, MGC delivers quality products and services while contributing to economic development and community well-being.

Driven by professionalism, integrity, and a forward-looking approach, Mansoorah Group of Companies strives to maintain the highest standards of performance.

Today, MGC stands as a dynamic organization dedicated to business excellence, operational efficiency, and responsible corporate leadership.
